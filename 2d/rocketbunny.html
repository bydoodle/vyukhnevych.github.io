The following logo was made for "Rocket Bunny," an online retailer of sexual items. My intention was to provide a suitable "spicy" element without using profanity. I came up with the following design by experimenting with colors, shapes, and letter formatting. The word "Rocket" is designed with sensual violet shapes and white accents, giving it a vibrant, palpable appearance. Conversely, "Bunny" is showcased in a bright manner with blue hues, bringing to mind the vibe of nightclubs and other adult-oriented venues.
