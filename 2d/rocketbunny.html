The next logo was created for an online store of adult products called "Rocket Bunny." I wanted to add an appropriate "spicy" touch without resorting to vulgarity. Playing with colors, shapes, and letter styling led me to the following design. The word "Rocket" is crafted in voluptuous forms in violet with white highlights, resembling something lively and tangible. On the other hand, "Bunny" is presented in a neon style in shades of blue, evoking the mood of nightclubs and similar adult-oriented establishments.
