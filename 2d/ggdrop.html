Banners for the social media of an online store. It took quite a bit of time to find materials for this work, but it was worth it. The work turned out exactly as I wanted, one of those cases when everything went according to plan. Holding the image in my mind, I did what was required, the client liked it, and everyone was satisfied. I spent the most time selecting the right colors - harmonious, not eye-piercing, non-toxic neon. After that, it was a matter of assembling everything in Photoshop in pieces, and that's what you see.
