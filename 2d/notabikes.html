This logo was created during work on one of the university projects. The essence of the task was to develop a fictional brand with a non-traditional idea. I proposed the concept of establishing a company/business specializing in the production of custom bicycles, scooters, and skateboards made from old spare parts. The naming idea was borrowed from The Boring Company, which sold flamethrowers under the name "Not a Flamethrower." Since the target audience for our imaginary business was primarily young people, I chose a corresponding style for the logo. The font resembled graffiti, in black and white, against the backdrop of a vector skateboard.
