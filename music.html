On this page u can explore some of the songs that was created during work on some game projects. I always trying my best to create songs which will convey the mood of the game and accompany the player in the right mood. I also have a lot more works on my soundcloud which cannot be published here.
This song was written during a 48-hour game jam that I participated in with a group of four other people. I have never written music for a video game before. The main menu plays this music. It portrays a feeling of gloom and terror, even though the game itself isn't that scary.
This song was written as part of the same project as the preceding one. The game plays during gameplay. I was able to create the right rhythm that blended in perfectly with the game with the help of a straightforward amen break. The goal of the game is to hide from the locals and keep them awake, and this 8-bit tune goes perfectly with that. The tune captures a stealthy vibe. Owing to a lack of time and other challenges, I was forced to create a loop of this straightforward song.
