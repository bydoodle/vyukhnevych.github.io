I've made the decision to design my own 3D guitar. I've put a lot of effort into developing a solid topology and providing as many features as I can without going over an astronomical number of polygons. After a lot of UV tweaking, I eventually imported the Blender-created model into Substance Painter and painted it. I worked pretty hard on this project, and I'm happy with the outcome.
