I made this as part of a project for my university. Initially, I came across some fascinating references on "Candle eye" on Pinterest. I later had to draw out the scenario and then replicate it in Blender. I chose to animate this, and after eight hours of rendering, the result is this. Creating a realistic light and texturing books was an intriguing experience. I also gained knowledge of geometry nodes and the "randomization" features of various programs.
